well, hello there.

I'm Erika, a web designer and developer from Seattle, Washington. I make websites and digital communications that look great, function perfectly, and make my clients more money.

Before embracing my inner tech nerd, I worked in philanthropy for over a decade, investing in people, projects, and communities to make our world a better place. I'm super-lucky to still consult part-time to a few of the best organizations out there.

When I'm not wireframing, writing code, or managing nonprofit programs, you can find me skiing, drinking kombucha, or reading detective novels. Or—my latest obsession—surfing. So fun! Even in full wetsuit getup in the Pacific Northwest's 50° waters.
